What makes our coffe special

A love and passion for coffee

Home of the Bean creates an experience that not only allows its customers to enjoy their coffee but educate them about how it is made and the fair trade that goes into creating each brew and confectionary. Our beans are sourced from different roasteries and as far away as Burundi, Ethiopia, Costa Rica and Guatemala. At Home of the Bean, our aim has always been to work with different roasters, across the world, and home the best beans in our hoppers (not just any beans, thee Bean) and that's where our name came about.
